📜 How to Play Guide

Your goal is to guess the secret number in the allowed attempts (Hearts ❤️).

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy Level: 1-50, 10 attempts (Reward: 50🪙)
  • Medium Level: 1-100, 7 attempts (Reward: 100🪙)
  • Hard Level: 1-250, 5 attempts (Reward: 200🪙)
  • Expert Level: 1-500, 3 attempts (Reward: 500🪙)

Hints & Tools (Shop):

New accounts start with 2 free uses of each hint/tool. Use them in matches subject to level usage limits.

  • Odd/Even Hint: Checks if secret number is Odd or Even.
    • Easy Limit: 1/match | Medium Limit: 2/match | Hard Limit: 3/match | Expert Limit: 4/match.
  • Range Hint: Narrows the guessing range.
    • Easy Limit: 1/match | Medium Limit: 2/match | Hard Limit: 3/match | Expert Limit: 4/match.
  • Extra Life (+1 Heart): Restores 1 Heart.
    • Easy/Medium Limit: 1/match | Hard/Expert Limit: 2/match.
  • Extra Time (+10s): Adds 10 seconds.
    • Easy Limit: 2/match | Medium Limit: 3/match | Hard Limit: 4/match | Expert Limit: 5/match.
